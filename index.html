Let's Compare Notes
Sharing Experiences in User Experience

The user experience field owes its success and longevity to the diversity of the people that it's comprised of. For IA Summit 2016's Poster Night (something you should come to), we want to explore and distill a small facet of our collective diversity to share and discuss with our community.

So let's talk about our experiences — our Yesterday, where we've been; our Today, where we are now; and our Tomorrow, where we think and want to go.
